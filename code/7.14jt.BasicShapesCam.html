Please use a browser that supports "canvas"

Use WASD keys to move around and arrow keys to rotate camera

The stationary red, green, and blue lines correspond to the x, y, and z axes respectively

The moving red, green, and blue lines correspond to the local x, y, and z axes respectively for that joint

Drag the mouse horizontally to rotate the center crystal around the vertical axis

Drag the mouse vertically to rotate the center crystal around a horizontal axis perpendicular to the camera's aim
